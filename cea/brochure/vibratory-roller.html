VIBRATORY ROLLER
10.DL.1000/1000-C

Features:

The 12.CV Vibratory Roller is an attachment easily installed on any skid steer loader on the market, increasing the versatility, and performance of your machine.

The 12.CV is a well thought out accessory used for conservation and repair jobs. Not only can it be used for the compaction of sidewalks, ditches, walkways and sporting installations, but also for the preparation of small areas to be asphalted.

The 12.CV allows for the compaction of places that are difficult to access with other types of compaction systems.

Optional Equipment

  • 26 Gal. Sprinker System
  • Removable Sheeps Foot Segments
