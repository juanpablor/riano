ABOUT US

SomoS Design and Construction, Inc. is a Montreal based company. Our unparalleled customer service and drive for success, have earned us an outstanding reputation as a reliable partner in project management, design, renovation, and turnkey solutions.

We set the bar for quality workmanship and environmentally friendly policies. Our professional team will efficiently and skillfully bring your projects to term, all while respecting your budget and your schedule.

Developing trustworthy relationships with our clients is at the core of everything we do.

We are customer oriented, we don’t only work for you, we work with you providing personalized service and attention
